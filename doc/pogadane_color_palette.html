1. Logo Usage

App Icon

Used for favicons, app launchers, and avatars.

Horizontal Logotype

Primary logo for headers and marketing.

Logotype Animation

Used for loading screens or promotional material.

Vertical Logotype

For use in space-constrained vertical layouts.

Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Give the logo ample clear space.
  • Use on light or white backgrounds for best contrast.
  • Ensure the logotype text is legible.

Don't

  • Distort, stretch, or rotate the logo.
  • Change the logo's colors.
  • Place on busy backgrounds that reduce clarity.
  • Use the icon and logotype right next to each other (unless part of the header).
